African Girls Can Code Initiative (AGCCI)

Welcome to the AGCCI Community

Welcome to the official community page for the African Girls Can Code Initiative (AGCCI). This initiative, in partnership with UN Women, the African Union Commission, and others, is dedicated to empowering young women and girls across Africa with the digital skills they need to become innovators and leaders in the tech sector.

Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing hands-on training in coding, robotics, and other key digital skills. We believe that by equipping the next generation with these tools, we can drive social change and build a more inclusive digital future for the entire continent.
